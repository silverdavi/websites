Research Milestone
ACCEPTED — ORAL PRESENTATION
"Not a high-level opinion piece but a rigorous analysis... includes a formal incompatibility result demonstrating potential failures of average metrics like AUC. A significant and timely contribution to building and validating clinically trusted AI."

The paper addresses a critical gap: while AI shows promise in healthcare, naive application of standard ML metrics can mask systematic failures. We formalize when and how AI evaluation breaks down in clinical settings — and what to do about it.
