## Domains

### unpop.info

**Type:** Book site  
**Status:** In development  
**Tagline:** A return to the roots of scientific wonder  
**Concept:** Academic subversion — rigorous journal aesthetic with personality

Site for "Unpopular Science" — a 50-chapter book spanning mathematics, physics, computer science, chemistry, philosophy, and history. Anti-sensationalist science communication that respects reader intelligence.

**Philosophy:** Science doesn't need manufactured excitement. Wonder should arise naturally from the ideas themselves. Rigorous accessibility — present science as it truly is, both elegant and complex.

**Sample chapters:**
- Why gold is yellow (relativistic quantum mechanics)
- Why apples fall (gravity as time dilation, not force)  
- Trees are made of air (95% mass from CO₂)
- Spectre/Meltdown (CPUs predicting the future leak secrets)
- A 4chan post that proved a math conjecture

**Tone:** Rigorous science communication  
**Audience:** Readers interested in unconventional scientific perspectives  
**Data:** `data/unpopular_science.json`

**Design:**

**Style notes:**
